Global Sales Intelligence & Executive DashboardProject OverviewThis repository contains a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution developed as part of the DSA 3050A - Business Intelligence & Data Visualization course at USIU-Africa. The project follows a full end-to-end BI workflow to solve a real-world organizational problem using Power BI.Business ScenarioAs a Business Intelligence Analyst, I have sourced and transformed a real-world sales dataset to provide actionable insights into regional performance, profit trends, and product distribution. The goal is to support executive decision-making through an interactive, data-driven narrative.Key BI Workflow Stages1. Data Sourcing & Preparation (Part A)Dataset: Sourced a high-fidelity sales dataset from Kaggle containing 10,000 rows.Transformations: Performed extensive cleaning in Power Query, including:Correcting data types and handling inconsistent entries.Removing duplicates to ensure data integrity.Creating derived columns such as VAT (16%).Extracting Year, Month, and Quarter attributes for time-series analysis.Technical Evidence: Maintained at least 10 Applied Steps per major table.2. Data Modelling (Part B)Architecture: Implemented a Star Schema structure to optimize performance and analytical clarity.Relationships: Established clean One-to-Many (1:*) relationships between the Fact table and Dimension tables.Integrity: Avoided many-to-many relationships and ensured proper filter directions for accurate cross-filtering.3. Dashboard Development (Part C)The final dashboard provides high-level summaries and granular drill-down capabilities:KPI Summary: Real-time tracking of Total Revenue, Profit, and Units Sold.Trend Analysis: Monthly and yearly profit trends.Comparative Analysis: Sales distribution across different regions and product categories.Interactivity: Fully integrated slicers and cross-filtering for dynamic data exploration.4.
